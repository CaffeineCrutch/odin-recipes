Cob Loaf

Popular, Filling, Cheesey!

Ingredients

  • 1 500g cob loaf
  • 1 small onion
  • olive oil
  • cream cheese
  • thickened cream
  • sour cream
  • grated cheese, Cheddar
  • white wine / moscato
  • shortcut bacon rashers
  • fresh chives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180c
  2. Slice the loaf 4cm from the top and cut into pieces
  3. Make a circular cut in the loaf and pull out the bread
  4. Toast the offcuts for 10 minutes in the oven
  5. Add olive oil to a pan, cook the onion and bacon for 3-4 minutes
  6. Add white wine and cook for a further 30 seconds
  7. Add in the sour and thickened cream
  8. Add the cream cheese and 1 cup of grated cheddar
  9. Add the chives and stir until smooth
  10. Once smooth, ladle the mixture into the loaf
  11. sprinkle some fresh chives and shredded cheese on top
  12. serve immediately with toasted pieces from the oven
