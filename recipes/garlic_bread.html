Garlic Bread

Fast, Easy, Definitely Tasty!

Ingredients

  • Italian or french loaf (not a baguette)
  • 1/2 Cup softened butter
  • Fresh garlic cloves
  • Parsley
  • Chives
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Prepare your pan and bread by lining a baking sheet with tinfoil.
  2. Slice your bread lengthwise, place it cut side up on the baking sheet.
  3. Make the garlic butter by mixing the herbs and salt together with the butter.
  4. Spread it evenly over the bread halves.
  5. Bake the bread for 10-15 minutes at 425 degrees F until golden on the edges.
  6. Slice halves into inch wide pieces and serve.
