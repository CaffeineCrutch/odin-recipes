Bruschetta

The Cold Weather Favourite

Ingredients

  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4 finely chopped roma tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • thinly sliced fresh basil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • 1 baguette or ciabatta bread

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to skillet with garlic cloves
  2. Cook until golden
  3. Strain diced tomatoes over collander, add salt
  4. Leave salted tomatoes to sit for 5 minutes
  5. Transfer to bowl and add basil vinegar and 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Add garlic and oil from skillet let marinate for 30 mins
  7. Preheat oven to 180c
  8. Brush both sides of bread with oil, cook for 5 minutes
  9. Turn bread and cook for further 5 minutes
  10. Rub cloves onto bread on one side
  11. Arrange bread onto platter and add tomatoes
  12. Dress with balsamic if desired and serve
